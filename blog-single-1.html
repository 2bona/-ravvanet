How to Secure Your AI Models with Fingerprint Technology

AI is rapidly transforming the way we live and work, but it also presents new security challenges. AI models are often trained on large amounts of sensitive data, and they can be vulnerable to attack if not properly secured. Fingerprint technology can be used to secure AI models in a number of ways. For example, fingerprints can be used to authenticate users who need to access AI models, or to detect and prevent unauthorized changes to AI models.
